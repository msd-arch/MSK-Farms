A small Islamabad farm with a big promise

We believe good meat, milk and eggs come from animals that are raised well. That belief has shaped everything at MSK Farms since day one.

Our story

From a backyard herd to Islamabad’s table

MSK Farms was founded by the Siddiqui family in 2015 with a simple frustration: it was hard to find meat, milk and eggs you could actually trust in the city. So we started raising our own — the old, desi way.

Today we raise four heritage breeds, deliver fresh mutton and dairy across Islamabad, and every Eid we help thousands of families perform their Qurbani with complete peace of mind. We’ve grown, but the promise hasn’t changed: raised with care, sold with honesty.

What we stand for

Our values

Raised the desi way

Native breeds, open pens, natural fodder and clean water — no growth hormones, no shortcuts, no cages.

Shariah-first Qurbani

Every Eid animal is healthy, of correct age, and slaughtered under proper Shariah supervision. You get full confidence.

Treated like family

Whether it’s our herd or an animal you board with us, each one is fed, watched and cared for by name.

Built on trust

We’d rather you see your own animal than take our word for it. Photos, visits and honest weights, always.

Our herd

The breeds we raise

Sahiwal

Dairy · A2 milk

Our reddish-brown Sahiwal cows are famous for rich, easy-to-digest A2 milk and a calm temperament.

Desi meat cattle

Qurbani · beef

Sturdy desi bulls and cows raised for Eid ul Adha — well-muscled, healthy, and grown to a proper Qurbani weight.

Talagangi sheep & dumba

Mutton · year-round

A hardy fat-tailed breed from the Talagang region, prized for tender, flavourful mutton all year.

Desi hen

Eggs · free-range

Free-roaming desi hens that lay deep-yolked eggs daily — the taste city eggs forgot.

Milestones

How we grew

A decade of raising animals, one season at a time.

2015

Two cows and a dream

MSK Farms started on the outskirts of Islamabad with a handful of Sahiwal cows and a promise: honest, farm-fresh food.

2018

Qurbani, done right

We opened online Qurbani booking so families could reserve healthy Eid cattle without the mandi chaos.

2021

Milk & eggs go daily

Daily doorstep delivery of fresh Sahiwal milk and desi eggs launched across central Islamabad.

2024

Boarding & fattening

We began keeping customers’ own animals — feeding and fattening them safely for Qurbani day.
